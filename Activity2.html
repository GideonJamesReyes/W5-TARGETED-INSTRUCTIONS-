COLORS OF THE RAINBOW

Red Red is the first color from the rainbow next to orange and opposite to violet. It has the longest wavelength on the visible spectrum of light.







Orange Orange is the colour between yellow and red on the spectrum of visible light.







Yellow Yellow is the color between green and orange on the spectrum of light.







Green Green is the color between cyan and yellow on the visible spectrum.







Blue Blue is one of the three primary colours in the RYB colour model, as well as in the RGB colour model.







Indigo Indigo is a deep color close to the color wheel blue, as well as to some variants of ultramarine, based on the ancient dye of the same name.







Violet Violet is the color of light at the short wavelength end of the visible spectrum, between blue and invisible ultraviolet.
